Introduction

Zoom Video Communications is a technology company that offers a cloud-based, peer-to-peer software platform for teleconferencing, telecommuting, and distance education. To reach a larger audience, you can leverage Brightcove Live to stream your meeting for others to see.

Steps

Follow these steps to host a conference to Brightcove Live:

  1. In your Zoom Meeting Settings, scroll to the “In Meeting (Advanced)” section and enable the “Allow live streaming meetings” option by clicking the toggle switch. If you don’t see this option, talk to your Zoom Account Manager about how to add this feature to your account.
  2. Now select Custom Live Streaming Service, you can optionally enter text notes in the box.
  3. The next step is to create a Live event in Brightcove Video Cloud. For information in how to create a Live event please click here.
    If you want to save the broadcast after it’s complete, make sure to check the Convert event to video asset when complete checkbox!
  4. In creating this event, you will have access to the Streaming URL and Streaming Key
    • Streaming URL - Streaming endpoint URL (You can find the endpoint URL in the Brightcove Live Control Room
    • Streaming Key - Stream Name (In Brightcove the Stream Name is always "alive")
  5. Publish and Embed the player for this Live event onto your website/intranet page, and take note of the page URL.
  6. Next, visit your scheduled meeting list and click the meeting you would like to stream.
  7. Scroll to the Live Streaming section
  8. Click on the configure live stream settings link. Here you will enter the Streaming URL, Streaming Key and the URL of the page where you are hosting the player.
  9. Next, from your Zoom call, select the MORE button, and click the "Live on Custom Live Streaming Service" option. This will start the Live stream.
